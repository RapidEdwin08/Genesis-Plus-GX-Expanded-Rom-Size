*** NO EXRomSize Support for SegaChannel *** (ONLY P4PR1UM Compatibility)

Emulator Instalation:
---------------------------------------------------
*Place genesis_plus_gx_libretro_android.so in any folder on your android device.

*In the main menu of retroarch, Select "Load Core".

*Scroll down the list of options and select "Install or Restore a core".

*Select genesis_plus_gx_libretro_android.so in the folder you placed it in.

*Enjoy Sega Channel Revival!!

*Extra: Be sure to map a reset key on your controller if you wish to quickly switch games.
